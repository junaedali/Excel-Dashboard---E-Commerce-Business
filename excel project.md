# E-Commerce Sales Performance Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project presents an interactive **E-Commerce Sales Performance Dashboard** created to analyze overall business performance across sales, orders, customer segments, locations, order status, and sales channels.

The dashboard helps management understand sales trends, customer behavior, top-performing regions, and channel-wise contribution in a clear and visual way.

## Dashboard Features

### 1. Category Filter

The dashboard includes a category slicer to filter performance by product category, such as:

- Electronics
- Fashion
- Health
- Other categories

This helps users analyze sales and order performance for specific product segments.

---

### 2. Division Filter

A division slicer is included to analyze performance by geographical division, such as:

- Barisal
- Chittagong
- Dhaka
- Other divisions

This allows users to understand regional sales contribution and customer demand.

---

## Visual Analysis

### Monthly Sales vs Orders Trend

The dashboard shows monthly performance from January to December.

Key observations:

- Sales increased from January to March.
- March shows the highest performance.
- Sales gradually declined after April.
- A slight recovery is visible around August.
- Sales decreased again from September to December.

This trend helps identify seasonal sales patterns and months that require stronger marketing campaigns.

---

### Men vs Women Sales

The gender-wise sales distribution shows:

| Gender | Sales Contribution |
|---|---:|
| Women | 64.05% |
| Men | 35.95% |

Women customers contributed more to total sales compared to men.  
This indicates that women are the stronger customer segment for this e-commerce business.

---

### Gender and Age Group Analysis

The dashboard compares sales performance across different age groups:

- Boomer
- Gen X
- Millennial

Key insight:

- Millennial customers show the highest contribution.
- Boomer customers show the lowest contribution.
- Women customers perform slightly better than men in several age groups.

This insight can help the business design targeted campaigns for young and active online buyers.

---

### Order Status Analysis

The order status section includes:

- Delivered
- Cancelled
- Refunded
- Returned

The largest portion appears to be delivered orders, which indicates a positive fulfillment performance.

This section helps identify issues related to order cancellation, refund, or return.

---

### Top 5 Cities by Sales

The dashboard highlights the top-performing cities:

- Chittagong
- Brahmanbaria
- Patuakhali
- Bandarban
- Barisal

Chittagong appears to be the strongest city in terms of sales performance.

This insight can help the business focus on high-performing cities for inventory planning, delivery support, and local promotions.

---

### Channel Wise Sales

The dashboard compares sales contribution from the top five e-commerce channels:

- Pickaboo
- PriyoShop
- AjkerDeal
- Daraz
- Chaldal

Pickaboo appears to be the largest sales contributor among the listed channels.

This analysis helps identify which sales channels are generating the highest revenue and which channels need improvement.

---

## Business Insights

From this dashboard, the following insights can be identified:

1. Total sales reached 21,176K with 31,047 total orders.
2. Average sales per order is 682.
3. Women customers contributed more than men.
4. Millennial customers are the strongest age-based customer group.
5. Chittagong is the top-performing city.
6. Pickaboo is the highest-performing sales channel.
7. March shows the highest monthly performance.
8. Sales decline is visible toward the end of the year.
9. Delivered orders dominate the order status section.
10. The business should focus more on high-performing customer segments, cities, and channels.
